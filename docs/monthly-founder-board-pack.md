# Monthly Founder / Board Pack

This document is step 5 of the company sale readiness plan.

Its purpose is to define the monthly review pack founders should actually use to run Autobazar123 well.

In simple words:

- the snapshot preserves history
- the board pack helps you make decisions

## What This Pack Is For

This pack should help answer:

- what changed this month
- what got better
- what got worse
- what needs a decision

It should be short enough to use every month and strong enough to support investor or buyer conversations later.

## How To Use It

- review it every month
- keep the structure stable
- compare it to the previous month
- compare it to the same month last year when possible
- add short notes for important context

Do not turn it into a giant slide deck.

The goal is clarity, not decoration.

## The Monthly Pack Structure

### 1. Executive Summary

This section should say, in plain language:

- what happened this month
- why it happened
- what matters most
- what requires attention next

Suggested questions:

- Was this a good month or bad month?
- What was the biggest win?
- What was the biggest problem?
- What is the one thing we need to fix next?

### 2. Headline KPI Summary

Include:

- traffic by channel
- searchers
- qualified leads
- confirmed sales
- active dealers
- paying dealers
- dealer revenue retention
- average revenue per dealer
- cash runway

Why:
This gives a fast summary of company health.

### 3. Buyer Funnel

Include:

- searchers
- listing viewers
- lead creators
- qualified leads
- confirmed sales

Questions to answer:

- Is buyer demand growing?
- Is traffic turning into action?
- Is conversion quality improving or weakening?

### 4. Seller Funnel

Include:

- new sellers
- listing drafts started
- listings submitted
- listings approved
- listings published
- sellers getting first lead
- sellers with sold listings

Questions to answer:

- Is seller onboarding easy enough?
- Are listings becoming useful supply?
- Where is the biggest drop-off?

### 5. Dealer Health

Include:

- new dealers
- activated dealers
- paying dealers
- dealer logo retention
- dealer revenue retention
- average revenue per dealer
- top dealer concentration

Questions to answer:

- Are dealers getting value?
- Are dealers staying?
- Is revenue too dependent on a few accounts?

### 6. Inventory And Liquidity

Include:

- total live listings
- premium listings
- stale inventory rate
- time to first lead
- days to sale
- inventory by city
- inventory by seller type

Questions to answer:

- Is supply quality improving?
- Which cities are strong or weak?
- Is liquidity getting better?

### 7. Revenue And Cash

Include:

- subscription revenue
- premium placement revenue
- refunds
- discounts
- ending cash
- monthly burn
- runway
- overdue invoices

Questions to answer:

- Is monetization improving?
- Are cash and collections under control?
- Are discounts or refunds becoming a problem?

### 8. Risk Review

Include:

- top traffic channel concentration
- top dealer concentration
- refund rate
- fraud rate
- duplicate rate
- moderation removal rate
- data quality issues

Questions to answer:

- Where is hidden fragility increasing?
- Are there trust or concentration problems?

### 9. Decisions And Actions

Include:

- top 3 decisions needed
- top 3 actions for next month
- owner for each action
- deadline for each action

Why:
Without this section, the pack becomes reporting without management.

## Good Habits For This Pack

### Keep It Honest

Do not hide bad months.

Use case:
The pack is most valuable when it shows the truth early.

### Keep It Short

Do not add dozens of pages.

Use case:
If people stop reading it, it stops helping the company.

### Keep Notes Simple

For each major metric move, explain:

- what changed
- why it likely changed
- whether it is temporary or structural

Use case:
This makes trend review much easier later.

### Keep Definitions Stable

Do not quietly redefine metrics between months.

Use case:
Stable definitions make trend comparisons trustworthy.

## How This Helps With Selling The Company

This pack helps in 3 ways:

### 1. Better Management

The company improves because the team sees the same important facts every month.

### 2. Better Story

When investors or buyers ask what changed over time, you already have the answer.

### 3. Better Diligence

If a buyer later asks for 12 to 24 months of management reporting, you already have a clean history.
