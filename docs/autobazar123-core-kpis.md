# Autobazar123 Core KPIs

This is the shortest important metrics file.

If you want to understand the business fast, start here.

If you want the simple monthly review sheet, use [Founder Dashboard Template](./founder-dashboard-template.md).

## The Big Rule

Autobazar123 is an ads marketplace.

It mainly makes money from:

- paid ad posting
- paid top / highlight features

So the most important metrics are the ones that prove:

- sellers pay
- ads get attention
- ads get sold
- sellers come back

Messages and internal leads are useful, but they are secondary.

## Primary KPIs

These are the main founder KPIs.

### 1. Paid Ads Posted

How many ads were successfully paid and published.

Why:
This is direct business activity tied to revenue.

### 2. Paid Feature Purchases

How many top / highlight purchases happened.

Why:
This shows whether premium placement has real value.

### 3. Listing Views

How many times listing detail pages were viewed.

Why:
If ads are not viewed, sellers will not feel value.

### 4. Sold Listings

How many listings were marked sold under the trusted rule.

Why:
This is one of the strongest proofs the marketplace works.

### 5. Time To Sale

How long it takes from publish to sold.

Why:
Faster sales make the platform more valuable to sellers.

### 6. Repeat Sellers

How many sellers come back to post again.

Why:
This shows whether the platform is useful enough to reuse.

### 7. Repeat Paying Sellers / Dealers

How many paying sellers or dealers spend again later.

Why:
This is stronger than one-time revenue.

### 8. Revenue From Ads And Features

Money earned from ad posting and premium features.

Why:
This is the actual business engine.

### 9. Seller Retention

Whether sellers and dealers stay active over time.

Why:
A business is stronger when customers return, not only when new ones arrive.

### 10. Traffic By Channel

Where visitors come from.

Why:
You need to know which channels bring useful demand and whether you depend too much on one.

## Secondary KPIs

These are still useful, but they are not the center of the business model.

- message sends
- phone reveal or contact actions
- qualified leads
- chat activity

Why they are secondary:

- they are only signals
- many real contacts happen outside the platform
- they do not directly describe how you earn money

They still help explain why sellers may feel value.

## Simple KPI Hierarchy

Use this order:

1. Revenue
2. Paid usage
3. Ad performance
4. Sold outcomes
5. Seller retention
6. Contact signals

That is the right mental model for Autobazar123.
