# Company Sale Readiness Master Checklist

This is the short practical checklist.

Use it as the main control page.

If something here is not done, the company is less ready to run well and less ready to sell well.

## Measurement

- [ ] Important objects have stable IDs
- [ ] Metric dictionary exists and is kept current
- [ ] Analytics event spec exists and is implemented
- [ ] Monthly snapshots are saved consistently
- [ ] Monthly founder / board pack is reviewed

## Marketplace Quality

- [ ] Paid ads posted are measurable
- [ ] Paid top / highlight purchases are measurable
- [ ] Listing views are measurable
- [ ] Sold listings are measurable
- [ ] Time to sale is measurable
- [ ] Repeat seller / dealer usage is measurable
- [ ] Dealer retention is measurable
- [ ] Sale-confirmed outcomes are measurable
- [ ] Paid placement and premium monetization are measurable

## Secondary Signals

- [ ] Contact / message signals are measurable if useful
- [ ] Qualified lead tracking is treated as secondary, not primary

## Legal And Ownership

- [ ] Company owns code and product IP
- [ ] Employee IP assignments are signed
- [ ] Contractor IP assignments are signed
- [ ] Important domains are company-controlled
- [ ] Important SaaS and cloud accounts are company-controlled
- [ ] Material contracts are signed and stored
- [ ] Corporate approvals and records are organized

## Finance

- [ ] Monthly close happens consistently
- [ ] Revenue categories are stable
- [ ] Refunds and discounts are tracked clearly
- [ ] Receivables and overdue invoices are reviewed
- [ ] Cash, burn, and runway are known monthly
- [ ] Finance and analytics tell a consistent story

## Operations

- [ ] Important recurring processes are documented
- [ ] Admin access list is maintained
- [ ] Founder-only dependencies are being reduced
- [ ] Key systems can survive transfer to another owner or team

## Diligence Readiness

- [ ] Diligence room folder structure exists
- [ ] KPI history is easy to export
- [ ] Founder / board packs are archived
- [ ] Legal and finance records are easy to find
- [ ] Top risks and concentration issues are known

## Simple Rule

If Autobazar123 can prove:

- people get value
- dealers keep paying
- the numbers are trustworthy
- the company owns and controls what matters
- the business can be transferred cleanly

then it is becoming a much stronger company to run and to sell.
