# Founder Dashboard Template

This is the simple monthly founder dashboard.

Use it to review Autobazar123 without drowning in metrics.

Right now this is a document, not an admin page.

## What This Is

This is the small set of numbers you should check every month.

It is designed for Autobazar123's real business model:

- paid ad posting
- paid top / highlight features
- listing performance
- sold outcomes
- repeat sellers

## The 10 Numbers To Watch

### 1. Paid Ads Posted

Question:
How many paid ads were successfully posted this month?

Why:
This is direct business activity.

### 2. Paid Feature Purchases

Question:
How many top / highlight purchases happened this month?

Why:
This shows whether premium placement has value.

### 3. Revenue From Ads And Features

Question:
How much money came from ad posting and premium features?

Why:
This is the business engine.

### 4. Listing Views

Question:
How many listing detail views happened this month?

Why:
If sellers do not get views, they will not keep paying.

### 5. Views Per Listing

Question:
On average, how much attention does each listing get?

Why:
This is a cleaner performance signal than raw traffic alone.

### 6. Sold Listings

Question:
How many listings were marked sold this month?

Why:
This is one of the strongest proofs the platform works.

### 7. Time To Sale

Question:
How long does it take from publish to sold?

Why:
Faster sales mean stronger seller value.

### 8. Repeat Sellers

Question:
How many sellers came back to post again?

Why:
Repeat behavior is stronger than one-time usage.

### 9. Repeat Paying Sellers / Dealers

Question:
How many paying sellers or dealers spent again?

Why:
This shows monetization durability.

### 10. Traffic By Channel

Question:
Where are visitors coming from?

Why:
You need to know which channels bring useful demand and where dependence risk exists.

## Secondary Numbers

These are useful, but not the center:

- messages sent
- qualified leads
- contact actions

Use them as supporting signals, not the main story.

## How To Read It

If these are improving together:

- paid ads posted
- paid feature purchases
- listing views
- sold listings
- repeat sellers

then Autobazar123 is probably getting stronger.

If traffic goes up but sold listings and repeat sellers do not:

- traffic quality may be weak
- seller value may be weak
- the business may look better than it really is
